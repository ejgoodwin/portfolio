Completing and Finalising Jobs

Before uploading a completed translation, please make sure:

  • that you have downloaded and applied the checklist provided by the client (if available)
  • that you have spell and grammar checked the completed translation
  • that the translation is provided in the correct format

We expect the translations to always be completed in a professional manner and to the best of your abilities.

Finalising Jobs

You should “Finalise” a translation job once you have uploaded the finalised files. It then takes this job off your ‘In Progress Jobs’ list and moves it into your ‘Finalised Jobs’ list. The job moves into the ‘Completed Jobs’ list when we at Straker have finalised all elements of the job with the client, which can take up to a few weeks. Jobs in the Finalised list will not count as active, so we won’t penalise your bids by suggesting you are busier than you are!
