Important update to LISA QA

5 January 2020
Category: High priority

Please note there has been a couple of updates to the LISA QA process:

  • Ability for QA Editors to mark up an error as a ‘GLOBAL’ error - to show that it appears multiple times in the batch.
  • Ability for QA Editors to mark up errors as ‘Preferential’ - which will not affect the score, as they are suggestions on how to ‘improve’ the translation, and technically not an ‘error’.

https://translator.strakertranslations.com/help/docs/quality-assurance/lisa-qa/
