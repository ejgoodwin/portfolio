Privacy Policy and GDPR Compliance

Collecting and Using Personal Information

Welcome to Straker’s websites. We appreciate your interest in us. When you visit and navigate our sites and when you communicate with us via our sites, we will not collect personal information about you unless you provide us that information voluntarily. Any non-public personal information that you may provide via our sites will be used solely for the purpose stated on the page where it is collected. In some cases, and in all cases where required by law or regulation, you will be able to update the information that you provide to us either by sending us an e-mail or, where you have established personal profiles with us, by updating your profile online. Please refer to the specific pages where data is collected for more information. Straker will not sell, license, transmit or disclose this information outside of Straker and its affiliated companies unless (a) expressly authorized by you, (b) necessary to enable Straker contractors or agents to perform certain functions for us, or (c) required or permitted by law. In all cases, we will disclose the information consistent with applicable laws and regulations and we will require the recipient to protect the information and use it only for the purpose it was provided.

By “personal information,” we mean data that is unique to an individual, such as a name, address, Social Security number, e-mail address, or telephone number, qualifications, years of experience and specialisms in order to help better target the work we offer to you. From time to time, we may request personal information from you at our sites in order to deliver requested materials to you, respond to your questions, or provide clarification over a job you are working with us on.
