Web App, Email Redesign & Styleguide

Feature project

I am working on an exciting project to redesign the internal web applications and client-facing emails of an international translations company. Their web applications are used around the world by their project managers, translators and clients. The UI was looking out of date, so we wanted to give it a revamp with our new branding, and look at restructuring to make the apps as user friendly and efficient as we could.

Deciding on a navigation was important, as some pages had their own page-specific menu as well as the main menu. I created prototypes in XD to show how the designs would work in practise, and then built static version of the top three so that the project managers could really get a feel for how they would work.

The emails needed a redesign to make sure they looked official and inviting. I wanted to bring in the new branding, so used the Straker triangles to form the background. Some of the emails were quite lengthy, so I made clear, clean sections to focus attention at one part at a time.
