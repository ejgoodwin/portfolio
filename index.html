Redesign, UI Development: Web Apps & Email

Feature project

I am working on an exciting project to redesign and develop the UI of internal web applications and client-facing emails of an international translations company. I designed and coded the UI, focusing on reusable components that can be integrated into the applications.

To encourage a focus on components, I introduced pattern libraries. I used Pattern Lab to house my UI components, and handlebars to include them in the pages. The components and pages can now be easily accessed by the backend developers when they integrate the new UI into our apps. It also displays our brand colours with their sass varaibles, and code snippets to ensure continuity across the UI.

Created at Straker Translations

UI example 1 UI example 2

The emails needed a redesign to make sure they looked official and inviting. I wanted to bring in the new branding, so I used the Straker triangles to form the background.

Developing the email templates was tricky, as I had to make sure they were compatible not only with different browsers but also different email clients. I used tables and inline styels to make sure the emails rendered correctly on all email clients.
